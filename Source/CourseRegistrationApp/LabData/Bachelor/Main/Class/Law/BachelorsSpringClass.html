LAW 8918: International Commercial Arbitration

This course provides an overview of international commercial arbitration and focuses with more specifically on international investment protection law and bilateral investment treaty revisions. The course will provide preparation for the International LL.M. Commercial Arbitration Competition, but it is open to J.D. students.

Credits: 3

Prerequisites: None

Intructor: Mikel King

LAW 8837: Negotiating Mergers And Acquisitions

Problem-method study of corporate, tax, securities, and business problems entailed in buying and selling a corporation; fundamental corporate changes: recapitalizations, mergers, sale of assets, sale of stock, and tender offers; corporate finance and rehabilitation of financially distressed corporations.

Credits: 3

Prerequisites: COMP-SCI 303, COMP-SCI 281R

Intructor: Tomy Yang
