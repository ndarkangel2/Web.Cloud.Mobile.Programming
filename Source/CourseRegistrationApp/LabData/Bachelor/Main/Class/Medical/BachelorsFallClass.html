MEDPA 5522 PA Professions II

This course focuses on the impact of racial, ethnic and socioeconomic health disparities on health care delivery. The student will become aware of differing health beliefs, values and expectations of patients and other health care professionals that can affect communication, decision-making, compliance and health outcomes.

Credits: 3

Prerequisites: None

Intructor: Mi Yang

MEDPA 5523 PA Professions III

This course focuses on the concept of licensing, credentialing and legal regulation of the physician assistant. Instruction will also be given in medical coding, billing and legal principles of documentation.

Prerequisites: COMP-SCI 303, COMP-SCI 281R
